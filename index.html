SERVICES:

At Tasty Africa, we bring the vibrant and diverse flavors of African cusine to your table.

BOLE AND FISH

Bole and fish is a beloved street food in Nigeria, particularly popular in the southern region, especially in Port Harcourt, Rivers State. It's a delightful combination of roasted plantains (bole) and grilled fish, often served with a spicy pepper sauce.

PEPPER SOUP

African Pepper Soup is a popular and flavorful dish that is enjoyed across various African countries. It's known for its spicy and aromatic broth, which is made with a combination of local spices and herbs.

EGUSI SOUP

Egusi soup is a popular and traditional West African dish, especially enjoyed in Nigeria, Ghana, and Cameroon. It is made with ground melon seeds (egusi) and is known for its rich, flavorful, and hearty taste.

ABACHA

Abacha, also known as African Salad, is a popular traditional dish from southeastern Nigeria, particularly among the Igbo people. It's made from dried shredded cassava and is enjoyed as a main dish or a snack.

NKWOBI

Nkwobi is a traditional Nigerian dish that is particularly popular among the Igbo people. It is a spicy delicacy made from cow foot, which is cooked until tender and then mixed with a flavorful and aromatic palm oil sauce.
